Have you ever felt lost?

Sometimes, even though you know where you are physically, you can still feel lost mentally.

Explore new spaces.

Traveling to new places will expose you to new ideas.

Taking roads less traveled will show you new experiences.

Seeking different planes of experiences will allow you to recontextualize those of your own.

When you give yourself space to be free, you can realize the restrictions that were placed on yourself.

"The greatest discovery in life is self-discovery. Until you find yourself, you will always be someone else. Become yourself."

- Myles Munroe
